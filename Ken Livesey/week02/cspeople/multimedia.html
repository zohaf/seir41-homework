Parallel compilers

Allen's last big project for IBM was the Parallel Translator (PTRAN), a system for compiling Fortran programs not specially written with parallelism in mind for execution on parallel computer architectures. She applied her extensive experience with interprocedural flow analysis to produce new algorithms for extracting parallelism from sequential code.
